Explore our destinations

Marsa Alam

Marsa Alam is one of the best-kept secrets on the Red Coast. Laid-back and easy-going, this gorgeous destination is idyllic. A seamless blend of turquoise water, golden sands, contemporary infrastructure, and a selection of fine resorts from the Jaz Hotel Group, make this the perfect holiday retreat.

Hurghada

Vibrant, youthful and lively, Hurghada showcases a dynamic aspect of contemporary Egypt, complimented by pristine waters and scenic views.

Madinat Makadi

Pristine, flat water and a steady breeze makes Makadi Bay one of the best kitesurfing and windsurfing destinations on the planet. Whether you’re a seasoned surfer or a novice, you’ll find the conditions are ideal at Makadi Bay.

Sharm El Sheikh

A sense of luxury, fine dancing, incredible waterscapes, and the essence of joie de vivre permeate Sharm El-Sheikh. Undoubtedly one of the finest resort regions in the world, Sharm has an undeniable charm that will bowl you over immediately.

Dahab

A former Bedouin settlement, Dahab evokes the mystique of Egypt. It’s name means “gold” in Arabic, a tribute to the velvety sands on its coast.

Almaza Bay

A sparkling backdrops of lakes, lagoons and landscaped gardens lead to the silvery sands and azure waters of the Mediterranean, that glitters in the sun. The name”Almaza” means “diamond” in Arabic and is particularly well-suited for such a dazzling landscape.

Luxor

Renown as the world’s greatest open-air museum, Luxor is a resplendent city. The ancient Greeks knew it as the City of a Hundred Gates. Situated on the east bank of the River Nile, it’s surroundings are unrivalled.

Ain El Sokhna

Ain El Sokhna is simply bliss. An oasis of golden sand, lush gardens and an azure sea, are juxtaposed against the date palms that sway in the breeze. Named for the hot springs that are situated at Gebel Ataka nearby, Ain El Sokhna is an idyllic escape.

Alexandria

Alexandria is known as the “Pearl of the Mediterranean” — one of the greatest cities ever known. With one foot in the past and another in the future, it offers visitors a delightful blend of ancient and avant-garde.

Ras Soma

Abu Soma is perched upon a peninsula that juts out into Kriasi Bay, overlooking the sparkling waters of the Red Sea. Whether you choose to take a cooling dip in the turquoise sea, bask in the sunshine, or get an energy boost with sports and activities, the choice is yours.
